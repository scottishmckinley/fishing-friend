BAROMETRIC PRESSURE

Target >1013hPa for settled weather. A falling barometer often triggers a pre-storm feeding window.

TIDAL FLOW (CURRENTS)

Predator fish use current to trap prey. 1.5 - 3.0 knots is the 'Kill Zone'. Slack water = slow fishing.

MINIMUM SIZES

Bass: 42cm | Cod: 35cm | Whiting: 27cm | Plaice: 27cm. Check local IFCA for specific regional variations.
