Rising (Flood): Water coming in. Usually brings predatory fish closer to shore as they follow baitfish.

Current Speed: Calculated by the "Rule of Twelfths." Flow is fastest at mid-tide (3 hours after HW). Slack water (no flow) occurs at high and low peaks.
